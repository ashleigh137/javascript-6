Bees are essential pollinators that play a critical role in ecosystems by enabling plant reproduction, with honeybees alone pollinating roughly 75% of fruits, nuts, and vegetables in the US. These social insects live in hives with a queen, workers, and drones, communicating through a specialized "waggle dance". Bees possess five eyes and are known for their high-speed flight. Unfortunately, bee populations are declining globally due to habitat loss and pesticides, making their protection crucial for environmental and food security
